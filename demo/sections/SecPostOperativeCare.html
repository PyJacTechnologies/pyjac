Post Operative Care

After Surgery Home Care – Client Story

“We couldn’t have chosen anyone better to look after Mum.” Mrs. M, an independent 75-year-old, had an emergency back operation which left her unable to use the stairs at home. Her doctor recommended Cavendish Homecare and one of our team arrived within 12 hours of her discharge from hospital. After assessing the situation, we arranged for Registered Nurses day and night to care for her. As well as dressing her wounds, her Registered Nurse also administered medication, organised clinical appointments, looked after her personal care and diet, and worked with her Physiotherapist to help in the recovery of her mobility. We also arranged for a hospital bed, with a specialised mattress, to be on the ground floor of her home until she was able to use the stairs on her own. With regular updates and frequent visits from the He Healthcare team, Mrs. M and her family were very pleased with the care and support she was given.



Why choose post-operative home care from us


Our person-centred approach means we go the extra mile to ensure everything is tailored to you. We provide an obligation-free home care assessment during which we discuss your requirements with you, your family and where appropriate your doctor and wider medical team. We create unique nursing and care packages that allow you to return home, confident in the knowledge that you have a Registered Nurse or Carer most-qualified to meet your needs. We are aware of the specialist needs of care for all operations and the complications which can occur during recovery, such as blood clots caused by inactivity. Our registered nurses can assist with taking prescribed medications and check up on how you are feeling, while monitoring healing of surgical wounds to spot any signs which may be of concern.
