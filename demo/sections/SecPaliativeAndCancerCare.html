Cancer Care

Exceptional home nursing when you need it most


At HeHealth care, we are experts in providing superior, specialist Cancer Care for people who need additional support after their diagnosis. We understand how difficult it can be trying to carry on with some semblance of normality during cancer treatment. Our team of Registered Private Nurses and Carers offer specialist clinical expertise as well as day-to-day assistance when everyday tasks, like showering and cooking, have become too overwhelming. We deliver bespoke home nursing services and care packages that are tailored to individual needs, which we know can make a world of difference when you need it most.


Whether it’s for you or a loved one, our live-in care is of superior quality. Our live-in carers and nurses can offer support and companionship throughout the day, and even during the night should you need 24 hour live-in care. Everything from doing the housekeeping and shopping to helping with personal care. They will be there to chat with and can help you get on with life reassured that there’s always someone on-hand to help.

Why people choose Cancer Care from us


As one of the leading providers of bespoke Cancer Care in the UK, we pride ourselves on offering superior support in a flexible way – with everything from day-to-day care to highly specialised clinical expertise. Our combined years of experience within the NHS and private healthcare sector means we can work closely with multidisciplinary teams to ensure the focus remains on what’s most important during this difficult time – your comfort and treatment. We offer:

  1. Flexible Support based on your needs
  2. A Registered Nurse or Carer to accompany you to or from appointments
  3. Someone to be on-hand during your treatment
  4. Support coping with the side-effects of chemotherapy or radiotherapy
  5. Assistance with rehabilitation following surgery for cancer
  6. Wound care
  7. Stoma care
  8. Guidance about charities and organisations who offer various forms of help
