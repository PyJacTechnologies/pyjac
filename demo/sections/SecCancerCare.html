Cancer Care

Exceptional home nursing when you need it most
At Cavendish Homecare, we are experts in providing superior, specialist Cancer Care for people who need additional support after their diagnosis. We understand how difficult it can be trying to carry on with some semblance of normality during cancer treatment. Our team of Registered Private Nurses and Carers offer specialist clinical expertise as well as day-to-day assistance when everyday tasks, like showering and cooking, have become too overwhelming. We deliver bespoke home nursing services and care packages that are tailored to individual needs, which we know can make a world of difference when you need it most.
