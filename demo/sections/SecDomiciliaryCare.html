Companion Care


Many older people can spend extended periods of time without having meaningful a conversation, which can reduce their quality of life.

If you, or your loved one needs someone to have chat with and keep them company at home or during daily activities, our registered nurses and carers can provide elderly companion care of the highest standards.


What is companion care?


This type of care is for those who do not need a lot of help but would like the companionship and support during their daily activities. This can have an emotional and cognitive benefit for elderly individuals due to the engaging conversations and reassurance when daily hassles or unfortunate life events occur.

Some ways our carers provide companionship:

  1. Suggesting engaging activities to enjoy
  2. Joining in on favourite hobbies and pastimes
  3. Accompaniment on daily tasks or outings to local events
  4. Getting to know friends and family when they visit or during group occasions

Personal Care


He Healthcare’s personal care services provide the highest quality care and support in the home. We want to support you to maintain your independence and dignity in your own home, ensuring you always feel comfortable and safe.

Whilst providing care of a sensitive nature our carers take your individual choices into consideration, maintaining your self-esteem is important to us when providing our personal care services.

We can assist you with (but not limited to):

  1. Applying creams
  2. Support with repositioning in bed
  3. Stretching and improving flexibility
  4. Dressing and getting ready for bed
  5. Applying make-up, and hair care
  6. Changing continence pads
  7. Assistance in shaving
  8. Bathing and showering, including bed-baths
  9. Helping you to the toilet, including using a commode or bed pan
  10. Oral hygiene
Your care requirements are tailored to your personal needs and routine, so you have the continuation of your normal schedule.
