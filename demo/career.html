Roles at HeHEalthCare

Career

You will need to have previous home care experience. A carer will be need to provide companionship for their patient, helping out with personal care and house keeping duties. This may include bathing, food preparation, and washing clothes. Along with these tasks, a carer may be required to monitor patients medication use.


Registered Nurse

For this role we expect up-to-date clinical skills, as well as home care or community care experience.
A registered nurse will provide exceptional care in the homes of their patients. This will include assisting with basic everyday needs and tasks. They will also be required to carry out specialised care, such as supervision of medication and monitoring of patients’ conditions – maintaining records, which can be communicated to health care providers. All our registered nurses should be friendly and responsive to their patients care preferences.
